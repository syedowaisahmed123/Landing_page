Designs & Works

Ghagra Choli

Ghagra Choli: The ghagra choli, also known as the chaniya choli, is a traditional ensemble popular in regions like Gujarat and Rajasthan. It features a flared skirt (ghagra), a fitted blouse (choli), and a dupatta. Opting for the ghagra without a blouse allows women to experiment with different styles of tops or even jackets, creating unique and personalized looks for festive events or weddings.

Floral Maxi Dress

Floral Maxi Dress: Embrace femininity and romance with a flowing floral maxi dress. Whether you're attending a garden party or a summer wedding, this dress is sure to make a statement.

Salwar Kameez

Salwar Kameez: The salwar kameez is a comfortable and versatile outfit comprising a long tunic top (kameez), loose-fitting trousers (salwar), and a matching dupatta. Choosing a salwar kameez without a fitted blouse offers freedom of movement and a relaxed fit, making it ideal for everyday wear or casual occasions.
